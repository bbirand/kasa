Idea to Prototype in Minutes

Use our interactive SDK to discover and interact with a large number of compatible devices. We do the hard work of talking to them. Use our reactive programming principles. 

# When temperature goes above 23C, turn on AC 
>>> (temp > 23) | ac.on

# Turn on AC at 6pm every day
>>> when('6pm') | ac.on

# Turn on/off randomly every 5 mins
>>> every('5min')| random_binary() | ac.on
